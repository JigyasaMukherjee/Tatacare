What are Implants?

Implants are small medical devices, often made from biocompatible materials, that are placed inside the body to deliver targeted treatment, support reconstructive surgeries, or help manage certain cancer-related symptoms. They are an essential part of modern cancer care, providing both therapeutic and supportive benefits.

Types of implants we offer?

  • Radiation Implants (Brachytherapy)
  • Breast Implants After Mastectomy
  • Orthopedic Implants
  • Drug Delivery Implants
  • Port Implants for Chemotherapy
  • Tissue Expander Implants
